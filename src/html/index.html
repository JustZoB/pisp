What is PISP

PISP is a portable programmer, cool and convenient

PISP is a portable programmer, cool and convenient

Portable ISP Programmer (PISP) is a pocket-sized device for firmware upgrade of electronics. The standalone programmer will be useful for ACS specialists for fine tuning and testing equipment in small-scale production, as well as in the field of service repair and restoration of devices.

  • Suitable for any ESP family boards
  • Stores many options for firmware simultaneously
  • Built-in 1300mAh battery
  • MicroSD slot
  • Compact, robust body and low weight
  • Easy to use even in the field conditions
